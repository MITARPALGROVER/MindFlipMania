How to Play Mind Flip Mania

Game Objective

Mind Flip Mania is a memory card game where your goal is to match all pairs of cards using the fewest flips possible.

How to Play

1. Click on a card to flip it and reveal its emoji.

2. Click on another card to find its matching pair.

3. If the cards match, they stay face up.

4. If they don't match, they flip back face down.

5. Remember the positions of the cards to make matches with fewer flips.

6. The game ends when you've matched all pairs or used all your available flips.

Difficulty Levels

Easy: 6 pairs (12 cards), 22 maximum flips

Medium: 9 pairs (18 cards), 28 maximum flips

Hard: 12 pairs (24 cards), 34 maximum flips

Scoring

You earn MF Coins for each pair you match:

- Easy: 2 coins per pair

- Medium: 3 coins per pair

- Hard: 4 coins per pair

Use your coins to purchase new themes in the shop!
